A Person Who Loves What He is Doing

Professional UseR, but still the AmateuR

2018 Dec 17

More than two years ago, 2016 November, I started learning R as part of a company push to be more data centric in our decision making. I found programming difficult to learn, but the opportunity R and data science offered encouraged me to continue pursuing it. Soon, I was able to apply R and data science to my work.

Last 2018 December 3, I started a new role as a junior data scientist in the research and development department. I use R everyday at work to analyse and interpret data.

The amateur is now a professional. I am not yet in the first month of my new role, I already have my hands full in data and R. I enjoy it, and I am happy to be someone to work with something that I love.

It will take me some time to adapt with my new role, not to mention my new shift, so I do not know how often I can still post. I hope to get my groove back by mid-January or early February. I will do my best to work with data and R as a hobby even if I work with R and data now for a living.
